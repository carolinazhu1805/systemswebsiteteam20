About Us

Our project builds on previous years UCL Open-Illumiroom V2 project. Our main goal is to implement triggers to this system allowing projectors to automatically display projections in relation to the triggers that have been added to the system. To achieve this, my team has worked on a User Interface which will allow creators to add triggers and also switch between immersive graphics mode and windows mode.

Our project video

Our Triggers

Here are the following triggers we have implemented:

Sound

Have projections synch up with sounds of your favourite games, movies and more

Colour

Colour analysis to help predict things such as daytime and nightime to time your projections with

Time

Add projections to specific time stamps for movies
